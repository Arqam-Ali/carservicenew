About Us

At Sanctuary Motors, we embrace our role as custodians of this proud legacy and uphold this responsibility in true British tradition: offering outstanding quality with unrivaled service.

Sanctuary Motors is good for Mercedes-Benz, Alfa Romeo, and GAC Motors across UK. Our regional insights enable us to deliver integrated, innovative, and superior service and solutions to all UK customers. At Sanctuary Motors, customers are assured of premium quality products, exceptional service, and comprehensive after-sales support.Our financial services department introduces innovative schemes tailored to custor needs and aligned with global policies and structures.

Statement

Sanctuary Motors Ltd has both medium term and long term strategy. In the medium term, the company aspire to offer engineering consultancy as well as offer customers an automotive dealership.

In the long term, the Sanctuary Motors Ltd aspire to set up in-house manufacturing capability to style and manufacture automotive body panels by utilising advanced design and manufacturing techniques and also to offer a unique dealership for a niche automotive industry.
